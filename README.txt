TPI ASPEN FORUM 2026 — LOCAL REVIEW COPY
Prepared July 4, 2026

HOW TO USE
  Open index.html in any web browser (double-click it). Everything is
  self-contained. Click through the top navigation and the buttons to move
  between pages exactly as they will work on the live site.

WHAT TO KNOW WHILE REVIEWING
  1. This is a local copy for proofreading and click-through, not the live
     site. Nothing here submits or saves anything.
  2. The Register page shows a labeled placeholder where the ticket picker
     will be. The real checkout is the Wix "Sell Tickets" widget, which only
     runs on the published site, so it cannot appear in a local copy.
  3. Speaker headshots and two location photos load from the web, so those
     images need an internet connection to appear. Everything else is included
     in the /assets folder and works offline.

WHAT TO LOOK FOR
  Typos, wrong titles or affiliations, broken or mislabeled links, anything
  that reads awkwardly, and anything factually off in the agenda, speaker
  list, travel details, or rates.

PAGES INCLUDED
  index, about, agenda, speakers, travel, tech-hub, register,
  past-forums, adjourned, seventeen-years
