Getting to Aspen

Three airports serve the forum, fly into whichever suits your schedule.
ASE Aspen / Pitkin County

The closest option, about ten minutes from the St. Regis. Nonstop service from Denver and, in season, several major hubs.

≈ 4 mi · 10 min drive
EGE Eagle County / Vail

A reliable alternate with more nonstop routes than Aspen. Rent a car or arrange a shuttle for the drive via I-70 and Highway 82.

≈ 70 mi · 1 hr 45 min drive
DEN Denver International

The most flight options by far. Connect to ASE on a short hop, or make the scenic mountain drive, one of the best in the country.

≈ 200 mi · 3.5–4 hr drive
The St. Regis offers an airport shuttle from ASE, contact the hotel at least 48 hours before arrival to arrange pickup.
Good to know
August in the mountains

Expect warm days, crisp evenings, and the occasional afternoon shower, bring a layer for the evening receptions. Aspen sits at 8,000 feet; hydrate on arrival.
