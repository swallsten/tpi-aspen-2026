# Editing the Aspen Forum speakers and agenda

Date: July 3, 2026

Everything you change lives in one Google Sheet. You never touch the website
itself. Edits appear on the live site within about five minutes.

## Speakers tab
One speaker per row.
- Name, Org, and Bio are the main fields. Title is optional.
- Category must be chosen from the dropdown in that cell. This keeps the filter
  buttons on the site working. Do not type a new category by hand.
- To add a photo: upload the image in Wix (Media Manager), copy its link, and
  paste it into the Photo URL cell. A speaker with no photo shows a plain circle,
  which is fine.
- To add a speaker, go to the first empty row and fill it in. To remove one,
  delete the whole row.

## Agenda tab
One row per session, in the order they happen.
- Order is just a number that sets the sequence: 10, 20, 30, and so on. To slip a
  new session between two existing ones, give it an in-between number like 25.
- Day and Date decide which day-tab the session appears under on the site. Keep
  the wording identical for every session on the same day (for example every
  Monday session says Day 1 and the same date), or the site will split them.
- Format must come from the dropdown. It controls the color label on the site.
- Speakers cell: put one person per line. To start a new line inside the same
  cell, press Alt+Enter (Option+Enter on a Mac). Write each line as:
      Name - affiliation
  A plain hyphen is fine. To mark the chair, add Moderator (or Co-Mod) at the end
  of that person's line. To flag someone not yet confirmed, add *Pending at the
  end of their line. Examples:
      Anna Gomez - Commissioner, FCC
      Bryan Tramont - Wilkinson Barker Knauer Moderator
      John Lin - House Energy & Commerce *Pending

## If something looks wrong on the site
- A speaker vanished from the filters: their Category cell is blank or misspelled.
- Sessions for one day split into two groups: the Day or Date text differs between
  rows. Make them identical.
- A change is not showing: wait five minutes and reload. There is a short cache.

That is the whole job. Add rows, edit cells, and the site follows.
